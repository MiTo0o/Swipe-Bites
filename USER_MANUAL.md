# SwipeBites User Manual

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Open the SwipeBites app at http://localhost:3000
2. Click "Sign Up" on the welcome screen
3. Enter your email address and create a password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Confirm your password
5. Click "Create Account"

### Logging In
1. Click "Login" on the welcome screen
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Using the Interface

### How to Choose Restaurants
- **Click the Heart Button**: Like a restaurant
- **Click the X Button**: Pass on a restaurant

### Restaurant Information
Each restaurant card displays:
- Restaurant name and cuisine type
- Price range ($ to $$$$)
- Distance from campus
- Average rating
- Popular dishes and specialties
- Dietary restriction indicators
- Website link

## Filtering Options

### Opening the Filter Panel
- Click the "Filter" button at the top of the interface
- Adjust your preferences and click "Apply Filters"
- Filters are active only for your current session

### Available Filters

**Cuisine Type**
- Select from multiple cuisine options
- Choose "All" to see restaurants from any cuisine

**Price Range**
- $ = Budget-friendly (under $15)
- $$ = Moderate ($15-25)
- $$$ = Upscale ($25-40)
- $$$$ = Fine dining ($40+)

**Distance**
- Set maximum distance from campus
- Options range from 1 mile to 10+ miles

**Dietary Preferences**
- Vegetarian
- Gluten-free

## Managing Your Session

### Viewing Liked Restaurants
1. Click "Liked Restaurants" in the navigation menu
2. Browse your collection of liked restaurants from this session
3. Browse your collection of ALL liked restaurant history
4. Use "Clear All" to remove liked restaraunt history

### Session Data
- Preferences reset when you close the browser or log out
- No permanent tracking of your location or personal data

## Troubleshooting

### App Not Loading
- Refresh your browser page
- Clear your browser cache
- Check your internet connection

### Slow Performance
- Clear browser cache
- Check if hardware acceleration is enabled in your browser settings
- Restart browser

### No Restaurants Showing
- Check your filter settings
- Expand your distance range
- Remove dietary restrictions temporarily

## Privacy and Data

### What We Track
- Your restaurant choices for the current session only
- Account information (email for login)
- Session data for maintaining login status

### Data Security
- Passwords are securely encrypted
- No personal location data is stored
- Session data is temporary and not permanently saved (except for restaraunt history)

## Getting Help

If you experience technical issues or need assistance:
1. Check this user manual first
2. Restart the application
3. Contact the development team for support

## App Information

- Restaurants are focused on the Cleveland State University area
- Some restaurant information may not be completely up-to-date
- Filter combinations may occasionally return limited results
- All preferences and choices are session-based only
